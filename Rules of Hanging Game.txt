Shiny gallows
You are about to be hanged by the ghost on a shiny gallows. 
However, the ghost wants to play a game with you before execution.
If you win, you can escape death. 
If you lose, well, have fun on the shiny gallows.

The ghost is asking you to guess a English word with 8 letters.
You have 7 chances to guess one letter or the whole word wrong.
If you filled each letter of the word or you guessed the word right,you win and escape the destiny of being hanged.
